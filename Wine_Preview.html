Introduction¶

Wine is an alcoholic beverage produced by the fermentation of the juice of fruits, usually grapes, although other fruits such as plum, banana, elderberry or blackcurrant may also be fermented and used to obtain products named "wine". In this short article the word "wine" refers to the product obtained from grapes. This product is probably the most ancient fermented beverage and was mentioned in the Bible and in other documents from Asiatic peoples. Exactly where wine was first made is still unclear. It could have been anywhere in the vast region, stretching from Portugal to Central Asia, where wild grapes grow.

Many consumers are interested in the quality and variety of wine. But also by manufacturer, country, year, description on the cover, popularity and, of course, price. In this analysis, you will find out if there is any benefit to wine, how many percent of the population consumes and which country is leading in the production of this drink.

2. Data description and objectives¶

As we know, wine is one of the most relevant and at the same time popular alcoholic drinks in the world. No matter how many years have passed, wine remains with the same great demand as before.

In this analysis based on wine production, you will find out how popular in the world,usually who uses, how much it affects to health, what is the highest price of this product and what are the ratings of manufacturers.

The main questions:¶
